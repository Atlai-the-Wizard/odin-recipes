Description

A hamburger in its most basic form is a patty of meat between two buns. It is a common American fast food dish, but that doesn't mean it is just junk food.

The idea is simple, but there are many options when preparing a hamburger. This lets you make your own perfect burger.

Ingredients

  • Patty (your choice):
    • Beef
    • Chicken
    • Veggie
    • That horrible pork/beef hybrid mix that Japanese burgers use
  • Buns
  • Toppings (your choice):
    • Lettuce
    • Sliced tomatoes
    • Sliced onions
    • Cheese
    • Pickles
  • Condiments (your choice):
    • Mustard
    • Ketchup
    • Mayonnaise
    • BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the patty
  2. Prepare the toppings
  3. Optional: grill the buns (highly recommended)
  4. Assemble the burger
    1. Apply lettuce/onion to lower half of bun (optional)
    2. Apply patty
    3. Apply additional toppings (including more lettuce/onions, if done in step I.)
    4. Apply healthy amount of desired condiments
    5. Apply upper half of bun
  5. Consume

Ramen Recipe

Home

Description

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle dish served in various flavors of broth. Common variants include salt, soy sauce, miso (fermented soybean paste) and chashu (sliced pork).

Ingredients

  • Broth (your choice):
    • Chicken
    • Pork
    • Veggie
  • Wheat flour noodles
  • Seasoning sauce
    • Soy Sauce
    • Miso
    • Salt
  • Toppings (your choice):
    • Meat
    • Egg
    • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Simmer broth
  2. Cook noodles
    1. Boil a bowl of water
    2. Add noodles to the boiling water, and cook until slightly soft
    3. Drain noodles, but do not rinse
  3. Prepare toppings
    • Meat
      1. Cook meat to specifications
      2. Add meat to broth
    • Vegetables
      1. Cook vegetables to specifications (you can do this outside or inside the broth)
      2. Add to broth if you didn't in step 1
    • Egg
      1. Acquire hardboiled eggs
      2. Either add to broth now or wait to serve with finished bowl
  4. Prepare bowl
    1. Acquire properly-sized bowl
    2. Add noodles to bowl
    3. Add broth to bowl
    4. Add vegetables to bowl if you haven't already
    5. Add remaining items of your choosing
  5. Consume
